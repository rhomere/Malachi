Roof Shape

Flat w/ Parapets (3+ feet)

This option applies to any roof with a slope less than 5º (rise/run ratio < 1/12) and with parapets more than three feet tall.

Flat

Flat roofs defined the same as the option above but without roof parapets or with parapets shorter than three feet tall.

Hip

Hip roofs, which have sloping surfaces on all four sides of a building, generally perform the best of all roof shapes during a windstorm.

Gable

A gable roof is one that is formed by two sloping surfaces that meet at a ridge. Braced gable roofs are gable roofs with cross bracing in the attic that extends from an interior truss to the bottom chord of the exterior truss.
