Submit
  • Risks with one or more Equipment Breakdown losses within the past five years
  • Medical Offices and Health Care Facilities with Total Insured Value in excess of $250,000
  • Total Insured Value limits that exceed your agency authority
  • Property classifications described as “Submit” in the Underwriting Manual
  • Risks with any single piece of equipment valued at more than $100,000
Prohibited
  • Any risk with equipment requiring a jurisdictional inspection and/or certification (i.e. boilers and pressure vessels, elevators, etc.)
  • Risks with high-value, specialized machinery such as MRI machines or Cat Scan machines
  • Inland Marine exposures
  • Manufacturing or Processing risks (ISO GL Classes 5xxxx)
  • Contracting risks (ISO GL Classes 9xxxx)
  • Equipment more than 25 years old
  • Property classifications described as “Prohibited” in the Underwriting Manual
  • Any account which generates electrical power, regardless of whether it is for private or public use
  • Equipment Breakdown coverage for car washes
